About us

BlueProof was founded in 2022 by Rafi Levy (left) and Max Turner (right). Rafi and Max met at Jesus College, Cambridge, and first worked together during their time on the 2022 Jesus May Ball committee. As a computer scientist and an architectural designer respectively, they saw an opportunity to combine their skills and created a companion application for that year's ball. BlueProof was born out of the success of the application and has since grown to become a full-time app development company focusing on the integration of interactive 3D models for the web.
